Manufacturing

each shoe is made up of three parts: the upper, the lining or sock, and outersole. each of these are made from varing matriels, but each will be labeled with universal symbols to inform consumer what material their shoe is made of. knowing these matirels can help you better understand your consumer's needs and better sell to their style.

upper

This symbol refers to the upper matieral of the shoe. It is the matierl that is first seen and felt when you pick up the shoe.

lining&sock

This symbol refers to the material used for the lining/sock. This the material inside the shoe is there to create a lining for the upper but also to bring support and comfort to our feet.

outer sole

This symbol refers to the bottom outer portion of the shoe. THis is the portion of the shoe that makes contact with the ground.

the three most common symbols for martiels used are leather, textile and other matrieals. Converse's most common other material will be rubber, which is what made them famous in 1908.

leather

The leather symbol represnets that the materal used comes from the skin or hide of an animal. There are many variations of leather so this symbol indcates that soem form of leather is being used. A few different types of leather are suede, nubuck, leather. It can be helpful to know this, becasue if a customer comes in desiring a vegan option then you are able to help them find a shoe that has no anmail product used in it's construction.

textile

This symbol indactes that a fabric was used. Converse's most common textile is cotton canvas. This symbol indicates that either man-made or natural fabrics were used in this material.

other materials

This symbol seems to be a catch all but this symbol just incates that it is a man-made or natural material that is not a fabric. Often this symbol is used to refer to the material on the outer sole, and Converse is famous for it's rubber soled shoes. Rubber is the most common other material used on our shoes.

upper

The upper is what is the portion that covers the whole top of the shoe. The most common upper on Converse shoes is a canvas. This canvas upper is lightweight and durable for any time of the year. Other popular upper textiles are leather, suede, rubber, nubuck-leather, peached-canvas, but you can make the upper just about out of any textiles. Converse has even found ways to reuse materials such as plastic, denim, and cotton to create eco-friendly uppers.

The upper is made up of different sections, starting with the vamp (front of the shoe from toe to laces), Quarter, and the heel.

eyelets,laces and anglets

The eyelets are the holes that run along the upper next to allow for the shoe to belaced and tied. On Converse, there are often also medial eyelets that enhance the airflow through the shoe. Eyelets can be made of many different materials, but most common on the classic Chuck Taylor are aluminum eyelets.

Shoelaces are one of the most functional aspects of the shoe, and with laces coming in every color you could imagine they also allow one to be creative with their All-Stars. The classic shoelaces are a 100% woven polyester to make for a durable long-lasting lace.

Most laces will have plastic in the types making it easier to lace up, but keep your eyes out for more unique styles will come with rose gold or some metal on the tips for style. The tips of the laces are called aglets. Unique aglets can help add the accent color to the perfect outfit.
